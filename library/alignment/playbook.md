# Alignment

## Index Line
> **Alignment:** Force the AI to explain your goal back to you to prove you share the same context.

## The Move *(ordered, wu-wei-atomic steps)*:
1. state the **goal**;
2. demand the Agent **echo the goal** back in its own words before executing;
3. validate the echo **Y/N**.
